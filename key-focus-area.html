Key Focus Areas

Our strategic focus areas guide SAYC’s mission to empower, mobilize, and advocate for youth development across South Africa.

Key Focus Areas

Education & Skills

We strive to improve access to quality education and vocational training. Our focus includes promoting STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) education to prepare youth for future careers. We also work on enhancing digital literacy and technology skills to ensure young people are equipped for the modern workforce - (Such as partnering with schools, colleges and universities for joint initiatives on career guidance and mentorship)

Economic Empowerment

Our initiatives support youth entrepreneurship and small business development, providing the foundation for economic independence. We facilitate access to finance and business, giving young entrepreneurs the resources they need to succeed. Additionally, we actively promote for internships and job creation opportunities to address youth unemployment working with all sectors.

Health & Wellbeing

We address mental health issues and provide counseling services to support the overall well-being of young people. Our programs promote healthy lifestyles and improved access to healthcare. We also focus on raising awareness about sexual and reproductive health to ensure youth are informed and empowered.

Civic Leadership & Governance

We encourage youth participation in governance and decision-making processes, fostering active citizenship. Our organization provides leadership training and development programs to nurture the next generation of leaders. We also promote volunteerism and community service, instilling social responsibility.

Environmental Sustainability

We educate youth about environmental issues and sustainability practices, fostering co-conscious mindsets. Our programs involve youth in conservation and climate action initiatives, encouraging hands-on participation in environmental protection. We also promote green jobs and sustainable livelihoods, preparing youth for the growing green economy.
